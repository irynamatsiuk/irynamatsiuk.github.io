My Learning Path

I had always thought that developers are from another planet. Just like acrobats at Cirque du Soleil, who do impossible things that I can never do.

This changed when I saw an advertisement for an online course "Introduction to Web Development" from an English project CFG. By that time I had already successfully completed CFG's course "Introduction to Data & SQL", so I decided to give it a try.

The Web Development course was mostly about html and css, that wasn't too difficult, so I dared to explore this field further and joined "Introduction to JavaScript" course, again from CFG. This is where the easy part ended. I understood that I need something more serious, than introductory course, that just scratches the surface.

After my research was done, I came to the conclusion that The Odin Project is exactly what I need. Self-paced, project based, with a comprehensive curriculum. I won't lie I had a lot of struggles and frustration in my journey and I still do have a lot! But here I am now grown from absolutely zero knowledge in web development to someone who has done some cool projects!
