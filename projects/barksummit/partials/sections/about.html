About Bark Summit 2025

Welcome to Bark Summit 2025—the world’s most tail-thumping celebration of creativity, collaboration, and canine-inspired innovation. We're not just throwing another conference—we’re crafting a playground where bold ideas chase frisbees of imagination, and every speaker has a bark worth listening to. Whether you're an artist, storyteller, or serial treat-giver, this summit is for curious minds who believe that inspiration often comes on four legs and with a wagging tail.

At Bark Summit, our mission is simple: Unleash creativity. Celebrate community. Pet more dogs. Through engaging talks, hands-on workshops, and fur-tastic networking events, we’re building a space where ideas run wild—and occasionally fetch a stick. Because when dogs lead the way, anything is pawsible.
